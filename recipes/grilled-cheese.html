Grilled Cheese

Description

This recipe is as simple as it can get. Two pieces of bread, with a slice of cheese in the middle.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • American Cheese Slices
  • Ghee or Butter

Steps

  1. Spread Ghee or Butter on one side of two slices of white bread
  2. Places one slice ghee/butter side down on your grill and top it with your desired amount of cheese
  3. After you reach your desired amount toasting on the slice of bread, place your other slice, butter side up on top of the cheese then flip
  4. Wait for the second slice to become nicely toasted, and then plate
  5. Cut in half diagonally to make two triangles
