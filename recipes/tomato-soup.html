Tomato Soup

Description

You know how to make grilled cheese. But do you really want to put that dry sandwich in your mouth? This is a simple 5 ingredient recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 large white onion
  • 1 can peeled, crushed tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups low sodium vegetable stock
  • 1/2 Tsp sea salt

Directions

  1. Melt the butter in a large saucepan
  2. Cut onion into wedges and add
  3. Add stock, tomatoes (with juices), and salt
  4. Bring to a simmer, and cook uncovered for 40 minutes stirring occasionally
  5. Blend the soup, and season to taste
