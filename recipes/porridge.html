Porridge

Description

Porridge is a very simple recipe, being cheap, quick, and filling. We will be making Whole Oat Porrdige with miso and avocado

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole oats
  • 4 cups water
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 Tbsp white miso paste
  • Half an avocado
  • 1 Tsp soy sauce
  • 1 scallion
  • Pinch of sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Soak your oats overnight
  2. Drain, and transfer to a blender
  3. Add the water, and salt
  4. Pulse until the oats are coarsely ground
  5. Pour into a pot and boil over high heat, stirring frequently
  6. Cover the pot, and reduce the heat to low, simmering for 30 minutes
  7. Turn the heat off, and stir the miso paste into the hot porridge until it is dissolved
  8. Transfer the porridge to a plate, and top with slices of avocado, soy sauce, chopped scallion, and sesame seeds
